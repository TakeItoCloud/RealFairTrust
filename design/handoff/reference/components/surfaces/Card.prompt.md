Surfaces and the metric primitive. `Card` floats content on the navy stage; `StatBlock` shows one performance number; `Avatar` is the consultant portrait.

```jsx
<Card variant="featured" padding={28}>…</Card>
<StatBlock value="4.9" label="Satisfação do cliente" gold delta="+0.2" />
<Avatar name="Sofia Marques" size={56} ring />
```

Cards use deep soft shadows and a faint translucent fill — never a hard border alone. Reserve `featured` (gold glow) for one hero card per view. Reserve the gold ring on Avatar and the gold StatBlock value for the single headline number — overusing gold flattens the hierarchy.
