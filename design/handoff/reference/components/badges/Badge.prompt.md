Status and ranking chips for the marketplace. `Badge` carries standing/verification, `Tag` carries quiet metadata, `RankBadge` is the leaderboard position coin.

```jsx
<Badge variant="gold">Top this month</Badge>
<Badge variant="rising">Rising Talent</Badge>
<Badge variant="success">Verificado</Badge>
<Tag>Apartamentos</Tag>
<RankBadge rank={1} />
```

Gold badges signal earned standing; the green `success` badge is verification only. `RankBadge` 1–3 glow in the bright title gold — everything below is neutral. Don't stack more than two badges on one card; let the ranking speak.
